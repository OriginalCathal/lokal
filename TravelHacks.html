Travel Hacks

Man must explore, and this is exploration at its greatest.

# 1 - Wifi - Find free wifi A.S.A.P, coffee shops are usually a winner.

# 2 - Holiday Days - Short breaks fly out Friday evening, back Monday morning, never use work holidays for weekend trips!

# 3 - Google Maps - Can be used offline by typing “OK Maps” and the visible area will save for future access.

# 4 - Get WiFi passwords by checking comments on FourSquare.

# 5 - Use tour buses to see the city but also as cheap taxis (hop on hop off).

# 6 - Hotels getting lightly renovated can result in up to 50% discount, keep a lookout on the hotels main site.

# 7 - City Bikes are in all major cities and are a great way to explore and get around on a budget. Most offer 3 day passes.

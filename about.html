About

DO MORE

Going on a trip and sitting on a tour bus is not my idea of a good holiday, endlessly snapping the camara at every building, every painting and sculpture in sight, is just a shit way to spend your time. If you're stuck behind the camera lense you don't get to truly experience a new place.

Most of the trips I take are short and sweet, roughly 48 hours. On these trips I will aim for three main activities, hit a few nice coffee shops, a brunch/lunch spot, a restaurant and finally a few bars. As wanky as it sound I will also share "Travel Hacks" I have picked up along the way. The aim is to make your trips more memorable, filled with fun, but more importantly share how you can go to a place and live like a lokal, and leave with a way better experiance then your prepackaged shit holiday.

"Buy the ticket, take the ride..." - Hunter S. Thompson .

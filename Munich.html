Munich, Germany

Oktoberfest

Oktoberfest in Munich with my girlfriend, 48 hours of beer, pretzels and Nutella crepes. Sounds like a recipe for success...

We wanted to do this cheap and usually when you do something cheap and last minute, quality gets thrown out the window! We ended up camping in what can only be described as a non stop college freshers week party... While amazing at first and the unlimited beer seemed too good to be true it ended up being a right pain in the ass with too much noise, terrible blowup mattresses and the god awful cold that shrouded the Munich forest.

We stayed in Stoke Travel Camping, which is a 5 min bus ride to the train station which brings you straight to Oktoberfest in 10 min. If I had given it a little more research I would stay in Mercure Hotel which is in the city centre, so no need for buses and trains to get to the city centre, it also costs the same as the camping which is great, but more importantly you will get a nights sleep and wont be surrounded by 'gap year' dickheads.

Stoke Travel Camping

Having never been to Germany I was super excited, but with such a short stay and having one main objective (Oktoberfest) it was going to be difficult to really get a good sense for the place.

We had three main objectives. 1) Oktoberfest, 2) See the Frauenkirche, and 3) , Hit the Marienplatz for the glockenspiel performance.

Oktoberfest

A 16-day folk festival running from mid September to the first weekend in October, with more than 6 million people from around the world attending the event every year. Tents are free entry, get in before 12pm or you will be standing, enjoy the traditional food, chat to others sitting beside you, sing and drink a shit load of beer. Also get Dirndl Dress or Lederhosen as its what everyone wears, and they look hilarious and add to the fun! Located @ Theresienwiese, 80339.

Frauenkirche

Beautiful cathedral that is considered a landmark symbol of the Bavarian capital city. You can climb the South tower and get an amazing view of the city and the Alps. Its only when you get in this place do you appreciate how big it is, and by big I mean huge, with 20,000 seating capacity! Located @ Frauenplatz 12, 80331.

Marienplatz

It has been the city's main square since 1158 and is simply stunning and reminded me so much of the Grand Palace in Brussels. Every day at 11 a.m. (as well as 12 p.m. and 5 p.m. in summer) it chimes and re-enacts two stories from the 16th century. It consists of 43 bells and 32 life-sized figures, grab a coffee from a nearby cafe and sit back and enjoy. Located @ Marienplatz 1, 80331.

Cafe/lunch

Finding a nice coffee shop is essential for me when I am traveling, you get to relax, catch up on emails, and grab that all important free wifi so you can find out where you are and let the family know you're alive. Munich is all about the beer gardens and taverns, which are everywhere so you don't need me giving any suggestions. For coffee hit Vits, really nice coffee and if you are interested they do barista courses. Also check out San Francisco Coffee Company they do a good brew and are located just around the corner from the hofgarten.

Hofgarten just around the corner from San Francisco Coffee Company

Dinner

The great thing about Oktoberfest is the food that accompanies the massive amounts of beer! We ate traditional sausage, Ox, Beef, Crepes, Pretzels and cheeses. We didn't need to venture outside the Oktoberfest tents as the food choices on offer where great. We did however eat in McDonalds as we happened to notice they sold the McRib! If you don't know what the McRib is then you haven't lived, I once wrote a letter to McDonalds asking if they could bring the McRib back to Ireland!

Night

During Oktoberfest Munich is buzzing with people, the beer gardens around the city are heaving and stay open till the early hours of the morning. The Oktoberfest tents close at 11pm but last orders are at 10pm. People worry about which tent is the best, I can safely say they are all very good and after a few beers you will be so merry you wont even care which tent your in!
